Imíq in idrinc - Food and Drink

It is important to note that in Lanwurq, most food and drink terms always occur with the definite proclitic i-unless explicitly indefinite e.g ibrí amijíqniq "I eat soup" and imaróqs aeqðm jistrían "i ate carrots yesterday". The definite article has been left out in the dictionary entries below.
